About Aakash


In the ocean of developing technologies, and a variety of electronic devices implementing those technologies, Aakash Mahindreker, got a chance to explore the depths at Vardhaman College of Engineering, in the year 2017. From learning how to print the “Hello World!” to visualise the accuracies of the prediction models and developing web applications in his undergraduate program, he took his first step in the industrial world as a Machine Learning Intern in December, 2019. Moving forward with his interest in Machine Learning and Data Analysis, he moved to Texas to pursue Masters in Computer Science at The University of Texas - Arlington, in January, 2022. As a graduate student, he got to learn the topics in Big Data Analytics as well as in Intelligent Systems. With the objective of starting a career in the relevant field, he is looking forward to gaining experience and developing skills with respect to learning what the world needs.
